Dennis Reynolds - The psychopath

Dennis Reynolds exhibits classic psychopathic behavior characterized by narcissism, manipulation, and a lack of empathy. He employs the "D.E.N.N.I.S. System" to emotionally exploit women, showcasing his disregard for their feelings. His actions often reveal an unsettling blend of charm and malevolence, making him both humorous and disturbing.

Charlie - The Dayman

Charlie Kelly otherwise known as the Dayman, is an illiterate and eccentric co-owner of Paddy's Pub. Known for his childlike demeanor and chaotic behavior, Charlie often engages in bizarre schemes and displays obsessive tendencies, particularly towards the Waitress. Despite his immaturity and questionable hygiene, he is fiercely loyal to his friends and brings a unique comedic energy to the group.
